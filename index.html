About NALD

The National Association for Local Development (NALD) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering communities and supporting youth. Through a wide range of activities and initiatives, NALD fosters social, educational, cultural, environmental, humanitarian, and health awareness across Lebanon.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive programs and activities that address the diverse needs of our community and youth.

Environmental Activities

Terrestrial & marine protection, awareness sessions

Social Activities

Health Activities

Awareness on alcohol, drugs, smoking, STDs

Educational Activities

Academic support, organizing offices, visiting public offices

Cultural Activities

Life skills, workshops, awareness sessions
