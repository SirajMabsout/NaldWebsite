Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with NALD?

There are many ways to get involved! You can participate in our programs, volunteer your time, become a partner organization, or support our mission through donations. Contact us to learn more about specific opportunities.

Do you offer programs in my area?

We serve communities across Lebanon. Contact us with your specific location, and we can let you know about programs available in your area or discuss opportunities to bring our initiatives to your community.

How can my organization partner with NALD?

We're always looking for organizations that share our values and commitment to community development. Contact us to discuss potential partnership opportunities and how we can work together.

What types of programs do you offer for youth?

We offer a wide range of youth programs including leadership development, skills training, educational support, health awareness, cultural activities, and community service opportunities. Visit our Programs page for more details.
