About NALD

Empowering communities and supporting youth through sustainable development initiatives across Lebanon.

Our Story

The National Association for Local Development (NALD) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering communities and supporting youth. Through a wide range of activities and initiatives, NALD fosters social, educational, cultural, environmental, humanitarian, and health awareness across Lebanon.

Founded with the vision of creating sustainable change at the local level, NALD works tirelessly to address the diverse needs of communities while building capacity and fostering resilience among young people.

Our Mission

National Association for Local Development (NALD) provides sustainable training for youth to become more productive and the leaders of the future by honing skills and learning techniques that align with the association's goals and the aspirations of young people and adolescents.

We believe that investing in youth is investing in the future. Our mission drives everything we do, from our educational programs to our community development initiatives. We focus on practical, hands-on training that equips young people with the skills they need to succeed in life and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Our Vision

We aim to achieve an advanced, sophisticated community free of impurities, capable of overcoming difficulties and challenges, and self-reliant.

Our vision is ambitious yet achievable. We envision communities that are not only self-sufficient but also thriving, innovative, and inclusive. By focusing on local development and youth empowerment, we believe we can create lasting positive change that benefits everyone.
