Our Affiliates

Working together with trusted partners to achieve our shared goals.

Partnership for Progress

At NALD, we believe that collaboration is key to creating lasting positive change. Our affiliate partners share our vision for community development and youth empowerment, and together we work towards common goals that benefit communities across Lebanon.

Through strategic partnerships, we leverage our collective strengths, resources, and expertise to maximize our impact and reach more people in need.

Our Trusted Partners

Youth National Association (YND)

Follow on Instagram

A leading organization dedicated to youth development and empowerment initiatives across Lebanon. YND focuses on providing young people with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to become active, engaged citizens and future leaders.

Areas of Focus:

  • Youth leadership development
  • Community engagement programs
  • Skills training and capacity building
  • Advocacy for youth rights

Our Collaboration:

NALD and YND work together on joint programs that combine our expertise in local development with their deep understanding of youth needs and aspirations. Together, we create comprehensive programs that address both individual and community development.

National Development Scout (NDS)

Follow on Instagram

A respected organization focused on community development and leadership programs. National Development Scout brings decades of experience in scouting principles and community service to our partnership.

Areas of Focus:

  • Leadership training and development
  • Community service projects
  • Character building programs
  • Environmental stewardship

Our Collaboration:

Through our partnership with National Development Scout, we combine traditional scouting values with modern development approaches. This collaboration allows us to reach diverse communities and provide comprehensive training that builds both character and practical skills.

Interested in Becoming a Partner?
