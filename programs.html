Our Programs

Comprehensive programs and activities that address the diverse needs of our community and youth.

What We Do

We provide a wide range of programs and activities designed to empower communities and support youth development across Lebanon.

Environmental Projects

Terrestrial & marine protection initiatives, awareness sessions, and community engagement activities focused on environmental conservation and sustainability.

  • Marine ecosystem protection
  • Terrestrial conservation programs
  • Environmental awareness campaigns
  • Community clean-up initiatives

Social Activities

Support to social welfare organizations and people with disabilities, promoting inclusion and equal opportunities for all community members.

  • Disability support programs
  • Social welfare assistance
  • Community inclusion initiatives
  • Volunteer coordination

Health Activities

Comprehensive health awareness programs covering alcohol, drugs, smoking, and STDs prevention for improved community health outcomes.

  • Substance abuse prevention
  • Sexual health education
  • Mental health awareness
  • Healthy lifestyle promotion

Educational Activities

Academic support, office organization, and public office visits designed to enhance educational outcomes and civic engagement.

  • Academic tutoring and support
  • Office management training
  • Public service exposure
  • Civic education programs

Cultural Activities

Archaeological site visits and cultural preservation activities that celebrate and protect Lebanon's rich heritage.

  • Archaeological site exploration
  • Cultural heritage preservation
  • Historical education programs
  • Traditional arts workshops

Youth Skills Enhancement

Life skills training, workshops, and awareness sessions designed to prepare youth for leadership and success.

  • Leadership development
  • Life skills workshops
  • Career guidance
  • Personal development training
